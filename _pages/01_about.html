Biography

Patrick Murphy recently completed his final year at George Brown College in Toronto. He has over 5 years of experience coding, and was hired by the Research and Innovation division of George Brown College as a developer for the Literacy Uplift project. This is a cross-platform application aimed at anyone who wants to improve their literacy skills. He is familiar with the Java, Python, JavaScript, PHP, C#, and Swift languages as well as Arduino, SQL, MongoDB, JSF, NodeJS, Angular, React, and Laravel technologies. In his spare time he enjoys playing guitar, finding new uses for old electronics, science, and comedy.
